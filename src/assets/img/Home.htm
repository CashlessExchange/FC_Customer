Frequently asked questions

How does Cashless Exchange work?

Members of the Cashless Exchange use trade credits instead of cash to do business together. If a member trades one thousand dollars of goods and services to another trade member, that member will receive one thousand dollars in trade credit, which can then be used to purchase a variety of goods and services from other members of the Cashless Exchange. Hotel rooms, resorts, restaurants, advertising, printing and many other goods and services can be obtained with trade credits. Cashless Exchange is a third party record keeper that sends monthly statements to each member reflecting their purchases, sales and current balance.

What makes Cashless Exchange unique?

The Cashless Exchange utilizes a unique, automated system that allows members to pay their fees and commissions with Gift Certificates making this the only barter exchange that is truly cashless.

How can I spend my trade?

Make a list of your needs. List everything you normally spend cash on. Do not limit yourself to one or two time item purchases. Use your checkbook as a memory tool. Also, think of your personal needs. If you regularly take cash out of the business for personal living expenses, travel or home improvements -- you should consider using the trade income you have accumulated, and save your cash for goods and services the barter network cannot easily acquire.

What if I don't accept the certificates from the customer?

You will be immediately barred from the program, and your account terminated. Furthermore, we will debit your account in an amount required to pay for any of your certificates in circulation, and other incidental costs. In additions, as we will need to refund money, and pay other expenses associated with settling and closing your account, you may incur an account closure fee among any other liquidated damages.

What if certificates are presented with the same code?

Don't accept them. Our system will only have one code per certificate, (if presented by paper certificate), or a flat amount if presented by card. All stored value is made invalid after usage, so each certificate or card balance is a one off instance.

What are the advantages?

Trade produces new business and allows you to expand your market. Secondly, trade conserves cash. Instead of spending cash to purchase needed goods and services, you can trade your own goods and services. Therefore, cash stays in your business for other purposes. Finally, trade lets you put idle resources to work. Excess time, excess inventory and excess capacity are converted into needed goods or services.

What does it cost to Join the exchange?

To join the exchange you will pay a one-time nominal cash fee of $100 and provide Gift Certificates to the value of $395 (which really cost you nothing and will help bring customers to your business).

How does the exchange profit?

Cashless Exchange collects a small fee on each transaction - 5% cash from both the buyer and the seller. These fees are necessary to maintain the operating costs of the exchange - but the good news is that there is no out of pocket expense for you! The system will automatically create Gift Certificates for us that cover yur commissions.

Could my rebate take longer than 30 days?

The short answer is: yes. However, if you refuse to redeem the certificate upon presentment, we will pursue all remedies of recourse accordingly.

If I don't sell any certificates do I have any obligation?

Yes. Your only obligation is to pay your monthly processing fees.
